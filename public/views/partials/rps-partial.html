The Rules

You are a Ninja Warrior. Gratz on that.

Your mission is to defeat the Robot Ninja Warrior that has been terrorizing the surrounding kingdom. Each round you have 3 choices

  • Punch your opponent
  • Block your opponent's punch and counter
  • Grab your opponent and throw him to the ground

Each round, your opponent will also select an action. If you both do the same thing, you counter each other's attacks or blocks and the round will start again. If not, the following will happen

  • A punch will hurt an opponent attempting to grab
  • A counter will block a punch and then respond with one of your own
  • A grab will pull a blocking opponent and throw him to the ground

After 5 rounds are scored the game is over and the person with the highest score will be the victor. Good luck young warrior!
